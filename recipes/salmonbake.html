Salmon Bake

Description

The beauty of this recipe is that it requires very little prepping, and it will still come out of the oven looking and tasting delicious.

It's a good solution when having guests over, or when a healthy yet tasty dinner is in order.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon portions
  • 500g sweet potatoes
  • 400g string beans
  • 2 purple onions
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic (more if desired)
  • 1 tbsp dill
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/2 lemon, sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200˚C, line baking sheet.
  2. Wash and slice sweet potatoes.
  3. Place potatoes and string beans on sheet and drizzle oil, lemon juice and garlic.
  4. Place salmons on vegetables and place lemon slices on top. Sprinkle with dill, salt and pepper.
  5. Bake 20min and check if salmon is done with a fork. If it flakes easily, remove salmon and bake veggies for another 5-10min until potatoes are done.
  6. Enjoy!
