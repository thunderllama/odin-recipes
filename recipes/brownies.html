Brownies

Description

This recipe will yield one small batch of brownies, serving approximately 8-10 portions.

They're easy to make, which makes them beginner-friendly without compromising on quality.

Ingredients

  • 145g butter
  • 250g sugar
  • 75g pure cocoa
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs (cold)
  • 65g all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Melt the butter
  2. Stir the sugar, cocoa and salt into the melted butter (still on the heat)
  3. Take the mixture off the heat. Wait until it is no longer hot to the touch
  4. Whisk the eggs into the mixture, one at a time
  5. Slowly whisk the flour into the mixture
  6. Line a baking tin with baking paper. (Note: The size of the tin should allow the dough to be about 2cm deep)
  7. Spread the dough into the tin
  8. Bake on 180o for ~25-30 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
