Garlic Shrimp with Quinoa

Description

This recipe yields 2 portions. I recommend using it as an entrée.

The simple ingredients make this a well-suited option for people with gluten or lactose intolerance.

Ingredients

  • 4tsp olive oil
  • 500g shrimp
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 1/4ts cayenne
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 large lemon
  • 3 tbsp parsley

Steps

  1. Heat 2tsp oil and add shrimp with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp chili. Sauté 3mins and transfer onto plate.
  2. Heat 2tsp oil and add onion until brown. Add garlic. Add quinoa, cayenne, 1/2tsp salt and 1/4 tsp chili. Brown 2min.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and bring to boil. Once boiling, cover and simmer 12-15min.
  4. Zest lemon into the pan, juice lemon and add parsley.
  5. Add shrimps on top and more parsley if desired.
  6. Enjoy!
